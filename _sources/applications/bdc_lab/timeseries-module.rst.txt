Time Series Features
++++++++++++++++++++

-   You can open the **time series** by clicking on **draw point**, draw a **point** with the cursor on the map and then clicking on graphic button on the sub-menu;
-   At the top of the time series settings window, you can see the collection list, below the the list of **bands** from the selected **collections**;
-   At the bottom of the land trajectory settings window, you can can select the start and end date of the search;
-   Inside the window, we present each of the band as a switcher with a **band name**;

Additional Features
+++++++++++++++++++

-   It is also possible to select bands from different collections, and view the time series with all **selected bands** within the search period;
